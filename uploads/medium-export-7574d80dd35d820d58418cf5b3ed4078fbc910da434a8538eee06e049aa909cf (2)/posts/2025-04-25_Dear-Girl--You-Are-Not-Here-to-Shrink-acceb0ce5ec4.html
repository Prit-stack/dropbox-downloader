Dear Girl, You Are Not Here to Shrink

Every girl has heard it at some point:
 “Be quiet.”
 “Be small.”
 “Don’t be too much.”
 “Don’t dream too big.”

But here’s the truth: You are not here to shrink. You are here to rise.

You are here to take up space with your ideas, your voice, your passion, and your light. You are allowed to be bold. You are allowed to speak up — even when your voice shakes. You are allowed to take the road less traveled and pave your own way if one doesn’t exist.

There is power in your softness.
 There is strength in your sensitivity.
 There is wisdom in your intuition.
 Never let the world convince you that you must dull your sparkle to be accepted.

You were not born to fit a mold — you were born to break it.

This world needs more girls who believe in themselves, who lift other girls up, who rewrite the stories they were handed, and who refuse to apologize for shining too brightly.

So go ahead — dream big. Show up. Take the risk. Be the first. Be the loudest. Be unapologetically you.

Because when one girl rises, she shows the rest of us that we can too.
